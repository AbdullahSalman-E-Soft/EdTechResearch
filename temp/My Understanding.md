# AO Feedback

AO-1
- This is the **"What"** of your essay. It measures your ability to understand the text and maintain a relevant argument.
- **The Goal:** Answer the question directly and stay on track.

AO-2
- This is the **"How"** of your essay. It measures your ability to look "under the hood" of the writing.
- **The Goal:** Explain how the writer makes the reader feel or think a certain way.

AO-3
- This is the **"Why"** and **"When"** of your essay. It measures your understanding of the world _outside_ the book.
- **The Goal:** Show how the time, place, and society in which the book was written influenced the story.
